Engineering College Facilities

Canteen

Our college canteen provides a variety of healthy and delicious food options. It is a spacious and well-maintained area where students can relax and enjoy their meals between classes.

Transport

The college offers reliable transport services to ensure that students and staff have easy access to the campus. Our fleet of buses covers multiple routes, making commuting convenient and hassle-free.

Hostel

Our hostel facilities provide comfortable and secure accommodation for outstation students. Each hostel is equipped with all necessary amenities to ensure a pleasant living experience.

Laboratories

Our state-of-the-art laboratories are equipped with the latest technology and tools for practical learning. Students have access to well-maintained labs for experiments and research activities.

Library

The college library boasts a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It provides a quiet and conducive environment for studying and research.

Sports Complex

Our sports complex includes facilities for various sports such as football, basketball, and tennis. We encourage students to engage in sports and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Gym

The college gym is equipped with modern fitness equipment and offers a space for students to stay fit and healthy. Regular fitness classes and personal training sessions are available.
